Hardware Concept

RE-CORE Architecture

A comprehensive conceptual framework exploring a Brain vs. Body philosophy for modular hardware. RE-CORE fundamentally rethinks how people interact with their devices by entirely decoupling the processing core from the physical chassis. Instead of discarding an entire system when its performance slows, it allows seamless, plug-and-play processor upgrades—designed to drastically reduce e-waste and build a more adaptable tech ecosystem.
